About us

Supplierscave.com is an online platform to connect suppliers and buyers of industrial products and services.

Directory: Directory contains information of products and services offered by various companies along with the Company profiles.

Stock & Inventory: Stockists can list the products available in their stock. Contractors can list the surplus inventory of left over material in this section. This section also has provision to list used machinery and items to be scrapped.

Rental: The rental companies can list the equipment and accessories available for hire in this section.
